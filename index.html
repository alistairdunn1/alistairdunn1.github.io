Ocean Environmental

Marine research and data science

What we do

Ocean Environmental provides high quality research and data science to support conservation and the sustainable management of marine resources. We are experts in marine and fisheries research providing mathematical and statistical services to ensure sustainable environmental outcomes

About us

Ocean Environmental is led by Alistair Dunn. Alistair has over 20 years of experience as a data scientist in marine environmental research. He started his research career after graduating with degrees in Mathematics from the University of Canterbury and Applied Statistics from the University of Oxford.

After leading New Zealand's National Institute of Water and Atmospheric Research (NIWA) fisheries modelling programme, he managed New Zealand's stock assessment science at Fisheries New Zealand (FNZ) for the Ministry for Primary Industries (MPI).

Ocean Environmental work closely with New Zealand and international organisations to review, research, and provide scientific advice to inform marine conservation and sustainable environmental management outcomes.
