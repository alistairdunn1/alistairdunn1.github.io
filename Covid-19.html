Coronavirus (COVID-19)

This page gives current summary plots of the Coronavirus (Covid-19) pandemic for New Zealand and selected countries. The data is sourced from the European Centre for Disease Prevention and Control (ECDC) (for confirmed cases from international data); from the New Zealand Ministry of Health (NZ MoH) (for New Zealand summaries of probable and confirmed cases); and from the Center for Systems Science and Engineering, John Hopkins University (JHU) (for recovered cases from international data). Data are typically updated daily and the images re-generated.

Note that the data from the ECDC and JHU may be different from that reported by the NZ MoH — the NZ MoH data includes both 'probable' as well as 'confirmed' cases, while the ECDC reports only 'confirmed' cases and JHU reports 'confirmed' and 'recovered'. Each of these data sets are updated using different timezones and hence daily totals may not always agree. In addition, the daily announcements from the Beehive on the number of 'confirmed' and 'probable' cases may not always agree with the NZ MoH released data for any given day due to the timing of announcements, cases where status may have changed, and when database updates are released on the NZ MoH website.
